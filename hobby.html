Talk to Allah ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ

This book actually talk about why do we have to make Du'a and also how to make du'a better. Ayesha Syahira highligt that du'a is essentials of ibadah just like wifi connection. Why? Because du'a is something we need in our daily life where it is necessity for our soul. If we did not practice making du'a, we will never go any deeper in having faith and will eventually breakdown.

After read the first chapter of the book above, I just cant imagine if I have so much feeling trapped inside and no one to speak about it, I will be collapsed and numb because my mind have become completely exhausted where not be able to express myself anymore.

The beauty of not making du'a is not just about the things that we desire and we make du'a for, but du'a also the things that we do not wish to happen or do not be taken away from us. For instances, the things that we already have, we have to make a du'a as we do not want that things are taken away. For someone who really lost her or himself and try to find peace in this dun'ya, I really recommend this book. You will see and feel the beauty and the wonderful of making du'a and will never stop asking more from Him.
